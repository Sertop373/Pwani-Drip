Our Story

From a Room
in Nyali.

In 2021, we started with a heat press, a laptop, and a burning belief that Mombasa deserved its own voice in streetwear. Not an imitation of Nairobi. Not a copy of Lagos. Something entirely, unapologetically coastal.

Four years later, Pwani Drip has dressed thousands across Kenya and beyond. Every piece is still designed in-house, still printed in Mombasa, still made with the same care as the very first tee.

We believe clothes are more than fabric — they're statements. Ours say: the coast is not behind. The coast is ahead.
